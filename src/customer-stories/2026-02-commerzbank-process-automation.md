## Background

Commerzbank is one of Germany's leading financial institutions, serving millions of customers with complex banking products and processes that span multiple regulatory domains.

## The Challenge

Process automation changes required multi-step handoffs between business analysts, IT teams, and compliance reviewers. Each iteration cycle took weeks, and the resulting implementations often drifted from original intent.

## How Interloom Helped

Commerzbank teams now define business process logic in natural language and continuously refine execution details as requirements evolve. Experts adjust workflow stages directly, and each action remains traceable and reviewable for governance needs.

The platform preserves the alignment between what business experts design and what the system actually executes, removing the translation gap.

## Results

Process design lead time dropped by 55%, policy rollouts happen twice as fast, and manual handoffs between teams decreased by 30%.
