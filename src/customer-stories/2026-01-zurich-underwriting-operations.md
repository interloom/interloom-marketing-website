## Background

Zurich Insurance Group is one of the world's largest insurance companies. Its underwriting teams handle thousands of complex risk cases each quarter, each requiring expert judgment, document review, and policy-aligned decision-making.

## The Challenge

Traditional tooling forced underwriting teams into rigid, predefined workflows that could not adapt to new risk types or evolving policy requirements without lengthy IT change cycles. As case volumes increased, the gap between operational needs and system capabilities widened.

## How Interloom Helped

Zurich's underwriting teams now structure workflows into clear, repeatable steps using natural language. AI-assisted extraction and classification reduce manual effort on routine tasks, while deterministic validation steps ensure that every output stays auditable and policy-aligned.

Domain experts can modify and extend workflows directly, without waiting for IT development cycles. Each update takes effect immediately, and execution context is preserved across every case.

## Results

The result is faster case handling with stronger transparency across teams. Triage time dropped by 42%, the team ships three times more workflow improvements per quarter, and every decision step is fully traceable.
