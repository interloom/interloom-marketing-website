## Background

Volkswagen Group Services provides shared operational capabilities to the Volkswagen Group's brands and companies, managing complex processes that touch multiple enterprise systems and specialist teams.

## The Challenge

Business process execution was fragmented across systems. Each tool held a partial view, and coordination relied on email, spreadsheets, and tribal knowledge. Context was lost at every handoff, and iteration cycles for integration changes took months.

## How Interloom Helped

Volkswagen Group Services teams use Interloom to connect process steps that span multiple enterprise systems. Operational experts define how work should flow, and agents execute repeatable tasks within controlled workflow boundaries.

New integrations can be built and iterated within a single day, and every action, decision, and handoff remains visible end-to-end.

## Results

Cross-system execution time improved by 60%. Each workflow now coordinates across four core systems on average, and integration iterations that previously took months now happen within 24 hours.
