**Global Search - April 9, 2025.** A new platform-wide search makes it possible to find any task across your workspace instantly. Enter a query and Interloom searches task titles, descriptions, and content, returning ranked results with highlighted matches so teams can scan and locate work quickly. Search is available from the navigation bar and by global shortcut, and results open directly in panel view so you do not lose context. Under the hood, precomputed vectors and a dedicated search service keep results fast at scale.

**Citations and Document Transparency - April 25, 2025.** AI responses now include inline citations that link directly to the exact passage used in the answer. Instead of generic source lists, each claim can be traced to its originating document chunk. This creates an auditable chain from response to evidence, improves trust, and makes verification easy in high-accuracy workflows. Files also expose indexing status - processed and searchable, currently indexing, or pending - so teams always know when content is available to the assistant.
