**Rebuilt Rich Text Editor - September 5, 2025.** Interloom's editing experience has been rebuilt on a modern Tiptap-based architecture for faster, more reliable writing in notes, task descriptions, and messages. Formatting and link behavior are more intuitive, and complex content - including entity references and inline mentions - now behaves consistently.

**AI Agent: Predictive Next Steps and Plan Execution - September 15, 2025.** Agents are now more proactive after each completed action, suggesting high-value follow-up steps and recommending the right specialist agent for each one. Agents can also execute structured plans end to end, turning high-level strategy into concrete, stepwise execution in a single interaction.
