---
title: Document Search
date: 2025-03-11
tag: Platform
---

Interloom ingests and indexes every document in your workspace automatically - files, notes, and attachments - and makes them searchable by your AI assistant. Uploaded documents are processed in the background and split into semantically meaningful chunks that agents can retrieve while answering questions or working through a case.

When you ask a question, the assistant does not rely only on the conversation thread. It searches across your full document library, returns the most relevant context, and cites specific sources in its response. For teams working with large volumes of reference material - policies, contracts, prior correspondence - this gives the agent access to the same institutional knowledge your team uses, without manual file handoffs.
